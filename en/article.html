The 5 Most Beautiful Beaches in Thailand You Must Discover

By Sophie Martin · June 12, 2026 · 8 min read

Thailand is famous for its postcard-perfect beaches, crystal clear waters and unforgettable sunsets. Whether you're a lazy beach lover or a snorkeling enthusiast, the Land of Smiles offers an exceptional coastline stretching over 3,000 kilometers.

1. Maya Bay — The absolute icon

Made famous by the film The Beach, Maya Bay on Koh Phi Phi Leh is arguably the most photographed beach in Thailand. After several years of closure to allow nature to regenerate, the site has reopened with strict visitor quotas.

"Maya Bay embodies paradise on earth. Its turquoise waters and snow-white sand make it a truly magical place." — National Geographic

2. Koh Phangan — Island paradise

Far more than its famous Full Moon Party, Koh Phangan has exceptionally beautiful wild beaches. Thong Nai Pan Noi beach on the east coast offers a preserved setting ideal for recharging away from the crowds.

3. Railay Beach — Karst adventure

Accessible only by boat, Railay Beach is a jewel nestled between spectacular limestone cliffs. It's a meeting place for climbers from around the world, as well as lovers of secluded beaches.

4. Koh Lipe — The Thai Maldives

Nicknamed the Maldives of Thailand, Koh Lipe offers exceptional marine life. Snorkeling is particularly renowned here, with visibility often exceeding 30 meters.

5. Patong Beach — Vibrant atmosphere

Located on the island of Phuket, Patong Beach is the most lively beach in Thailand. Ideal for those who want to combine relaxation, water sports and exciting nightlife.

When to go?

The best time to enjoy Thai beaches is from November to April, during the dry season. Temperatures range from 28°C to 34°C, with water at 27°C year-round.

Our advice

For a successful stay, allow at least 10 days to discover 2 to 3 different islands. Feel free to contact us for a customized tour in Thailand.
